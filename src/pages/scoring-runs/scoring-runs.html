Scoring Runs

  1. If a Preceding Runner is appealed for missing a base, it does not affect the base Runners behind him, whether put out or not, unless it’s the third out.
  2. Runs score if the Appeal Play for the third out is behind the Runner and is not a Force Out.
  3. Runs don’t score if the Batter is put out before reaching first.
  4. Runs don’t score if the third out on any Runner is a Force Out.
  5. Run’s do not score if a proceeding Runner is the third out on an Appeal Play.
