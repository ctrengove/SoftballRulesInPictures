Part 3: The Runner

  1. The Runner must touch first, second, third and home bases, in order, to score a run.
  2. The Runner may try to advance, or steal, another base after the pitcher releases the ball, but not before, or the Runner will be called out and the ball is dead.
  3. If forced to return to a base, a Runner must touch bases in reverse order.
  4. Two Runners may not occupy the same base. The first Runner retains the right to the base, except on Force Plays.
  5. If not a Force Play, and the second Runner is tagged while on the same base, he is out.
  6. Runners advance one base without risk when the Batter is awarded first base and Runners must advance to vacate first base for him.
