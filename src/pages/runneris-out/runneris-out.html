Runner is Out

  1. The Runner is out when he is tagged by a Fielder while off base.
  2. The Runner is out if he passes the Preceding Runner unless that Runner has been put out as occurs occasionally on “run-down” plays. The ball remains live.
  3. The Runner is out when the base to which he is advancing on a Force Play is tagged.
  4. A Runner is out when he is tagged with the ball while off base on a Force Play.
