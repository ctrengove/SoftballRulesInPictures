The Batter-Runner is Out

  1. The Batter-Runner is out if his Fair-Batted Ball bounces up and hits him while his foot is touching the ground and he is out of the Batter's box.
  2. The Batter-Runner is out if a Fair-Batted Ball is held by a Fielder on first base before the Batter-Runner reaches the base.
  3. The Batter-Runner is out if the ball is held by a Fielder on first base before the Batter-Runner reaches the base on a dropped third Strike.
  4. The Batter-Runner is out if he is touched (tagged) before he reaches first base on a Fair-Batted Ball, or on a third Strike that is not caught.
  5. The Batter-Runner is Not out if he drops the bat and the ball rolls and contacts the stationary bat If the bat hits the ball, the Batter is out.
  6. The Runner is out if he dodges more than .91 metres (3 feet) to avoid a Tag.
  7. The Batter-Runner is out if he interferes with a Fielder executing a play.
